# Do countries with more press freedom treat their animals better?

An interactive chart exploring the relationship between press freedom and animal welfare across 20 countries, created for [#30DayChartChallenge](https://github.com/30DayChartChallenge/Edition2026) 2026 **Day 6: Reporters Without Borders Data Day**.

## About

This visualization plots each country's [RSF World Press Freedom Index](https://rsf.org/en/index) score against its [Animal Protection Index](https://api.worldanimalprotection.org/) grade (B through E), using circular flag images as data marks. With a Spearman's rank correlation coefficient of **0.77**, the two measures are strongly correlated — though outliers like Mexico and Turkey show that the relationship isn't universal.

Built with [SvelteKit](https://svelte.dev/docs/kit), [Observable Plot](https://observablehq.com/plot/), and [Deno](https://deno.com/).

## Data sources

- [RSF World Press Freedom Index](https://rsf.org/en/index)
